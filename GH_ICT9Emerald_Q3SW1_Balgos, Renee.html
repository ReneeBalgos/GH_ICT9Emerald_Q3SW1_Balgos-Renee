Modern Family



Synopsis:

Modern Family is a sitcom that features three different families who are related to eachother. It shows how these families go through issues and problems individaully and collectively BUT they do it with their own little touch and comedy. This is a great comfort show if you want to laugh and have a chill time or if you want to feel comforted in general.

Characters:

The Dunphy Family (5)
The Pritchett Family (4)
The Tucker-Pritchett (3)
Claire Dunphy - She is the wife of Phil Dunphy and the mother of Haley, Alex, Luke. She is very competitive and controling, strict, and hates being wrong.
Jay Pritchett - He is the father of Claire, Mitchell, Manny, and Joe and the husband of Gloria. He likes to be in control and being right, though as he grows old he becomes more relaxed and laid back. The also only rarely shows affection towards other people but he tries.
Mitchell Pritchett - He is the son of Jay, the husband of Cam, and father of Lily. He tends to be uptight and worries a lot.
Phil Dunphy - Husband of Claire and father of Haley, Alex, and Luke. He always find ways to bond with his children. He sees himself as a "cool dad" and he is very child like.
Gloria Delgado-Pritchett - She the wife of Jay. She is a very loving mother and wife and person to her family in general.
Cameron Tucker - Husband of Mitchell and other father of Lily. He is a very dramatic and bubbly person.
Haley Dunphy - Eldest daughter of Claire and Phil. Is a "steriotypical" teenager and gets involved in trouble. Thinks social status is more important tha studies.
Manny Delgado - He is the son Gloria/ step son of Jay. He is very smart and mature for his age. He takes care of the people around him.
Lily Tucker-Pritchett - She is the only child (adopted) of Cam and Mitchell.She is known for her savage personality and iconic moments.
Alex Dunphy - She is the second child of Claire and Phil. She is known as the smart one in the family. She is an overachiever and thinks she is superior to everyone becasue she's smart. As opposed to her sister, she care more about her studies rather than her social life.
Joe Pritchett - The youngest member of the family. Son of Jay and Gloria.
Luke Dunphy - He is the youngest child of Claire and Phil. He is very goofy and playful. He is very child like and speaks/acts before he thinks.
